Stuff About Me

My name is Kaylla
and this is my data diary called "Daily Logs"

Everyday I have to write down a list of task I must do that day
inorder to remember everything and actually get stuff done.
My brain is super disorganized and keeping list stored in my
cranium usually means I'll get nothing done. I keep a journal
that works as an analog system to have all these list and more.
Inisde I also keep ideas, list of movies // books to complete,
a section to write down my thoughs (which I call Brain Throwup)
and many other things that just float aimlessly in my brain.
So here is a digital translation of my journal and brain.
